# The Spirit Animals of Go

Go is not merely a game of stones and territory. It is a mirror — reflecting the mind, the instincts, and the soul of the one who plays.

Every player carries a spirit within them. A way of seeing the board that is uniquely, irreducibly theirs. Some build empires slowly and without apology. Some wait in perfect stillness and then strike once, completely. Some weave invisible architecture across the entire board and let their opponent walk in of their own accord.

These are not strategies chosen from a menu. They are natures.

Each opponent you face here is not a difficulty setting. It is a living philosophy — a way of being in the game that has existed long before Go was ever recorded, expressed through the creatures that have always embodied it.

The board does not change. The stones do not change. Only the world you are playing inside.

Choose your opponent. Study their nature. The board will reveal your own.
